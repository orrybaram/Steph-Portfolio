About

Born and raised in the West Coast of Florida Stephanie Nicole has always had her hands in making. If it wasn't building from scraps around her grandfather's construction company, it was drawing up plans for the next big addition to the family's home. Now a recent graduate of Parsons The New School For Design's Product Design Bachelor Degree Program, Stephanie plans to stay in New York City and is excited for the next steps in her design career. With a wide variety of skills Stephanie is ready for anything and her drive and ambition will light they way.
